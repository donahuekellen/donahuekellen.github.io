Does Troop Rank Determine a Monkey's Sociableness?

Despite male2 being lower rank than male0 and male1 he is far more social than them, both inside and between families. male2 is in fact the most social of all the monkeys in the troop, which is reflected in his betweeness centrality which measures how often he is a node in the shortest path between nodes indicating that he is central to the group.
